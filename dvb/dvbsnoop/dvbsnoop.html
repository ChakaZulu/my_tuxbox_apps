dvbsnoop - A DVB Stream Analyzer Tool

What is dvbsnoop?

dvbsnoop is a DVB sniffer and monitoring program.

Its purpose is to debug, dump or view dvb/mpeg/dsm-cc/MHP stream information (e.g. digital television broadcasts) send via satellite, cable or terrestrial. Basically you can describe dvbsnoop as a simple DVB/MPEG stream analyzer, similar to TCP network sniffer programs like snoop on Sun Solaris or tcpdump under Linux.

The programm was mainly written to learn and understand the DVB protocol structures and streams sent via sattelites. Out of this reason, the programm ist not performance optimized, but it will hopefully do its job.
